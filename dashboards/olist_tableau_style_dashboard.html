Welcome back, Marketplace Team

This dashboard provides a portfolio-ready view of marketplace performance, tracking GMV, orders, customers, payment mix, reviews, logistics, sellers, and product risk from the Olist e-commerce dataset.

The layout follows the Tableau reference style: a dark executive header, clean white KPI cards, sharp blue accents, compact trend charts, and dense operational detail for decision-making.
